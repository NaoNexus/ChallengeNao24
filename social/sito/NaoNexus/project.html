Introducing: OneRetail

From small business to big corporation, OneRetail fits everywhere.

Welcome to the world of accessible retail.

Some stores can’t even afford a full time employee to sell their goods, though they would benefit from the investment.

With OneRetail, small business owners and large companies alike can benefit an extra worker with a small lifetime investment.

No hassles, no extra costs.

Custom tailored to customer taste.


With MorphCast’s AI model, NAO is able to understand customer taste and propose products that are akin to the customer’s taste.


What’s more? By profiling customer reactions to certain product proposals, NAO becomes better at suggesting products with every interaction..

Track customer sales, satisfaction and inventory.


With OneRetail’s webapp, retailers can track store inventory, sales and customer’s preferences towards certain products.


This way, business owners can always control their stock, which product is most sold and which one is most liked.

Our commitment to privacy.


MorphCast’s AI is fully G.D.P.R. compliant.
Customer data entries to the retailer’s database are always anonymous and never nominal.


Data is stored within a local database and never shared for data brokerage.
